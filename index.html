Jiahe (Grace) Liu

I am a second-year PhD candidate at the Faculty of Information Technology, Monash University, supervised by A/Prof Zongyuan Ge, A/Prof Levin Kuhlmann, and Dr Duong Nhu.

My research focuses on multimodal AI systems for clinical decision support in neurology and mental health, bringing together signals from imaging, text, and physiological data to build tools that support clinicians in real-world diagnostic and care settings.

Previously, I completed my Bachelor of Science (Data Science) and Master of Data Science at the University of Melbourne, where I was advised by A/Prof Mike Conway.
